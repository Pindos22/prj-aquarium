Regulations on ticket prices

Tickets include entrance fees and visits during the operating hours of the Aquarium, excluding other auxiliary services.

Customers must present tickets to visit the aquarium. Tickets cannot be transferred to others after purchase.

. Valid tickets must be intact, not torn, patched, or erased.

Purchased tickets are non-refundable. In case of loss, tickets will not be reissued.
